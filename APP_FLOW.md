## User Actions

### Onboarding
- Swipe left/right to navigate
- Tap "Next" to go forward
- Tap "Skip" to jump to end
- Tap "Get Started" to proceed to login

### Authentication
- Enter mobile number
- Tap "Send OTP"
- Enter OTP (1234 for demo)
- Tap "Login"
- Or tap "Sign Up" link

### Main App
- Tap bottom nav icons to switch tabs
- Tap cards to view details
- Tap buttons to perform actions
- Type and send messages in chatbot
- Scroll to see more content
